Pricing Model We Offer

#1 Dedicated QA Engineer

Our Quality Assurance Engineer will become part of your team and work exclusively on your projects. This model is ideal for long term projects, frequent changing requirements, scope and multiple projects when running parallel. Billing would be made on monthly basis.

#2 Time and Materials

Ideal for Client's when delivery of receivable are immediate. Our Quality Assurance Engineer would work on Client's project on need basis. Our QA engineer would have complete understanding of the Client's project. Billing would be made on hourly rates.

#3 Fixed Price Project

Ideal for Client's when the requirement is clear, no frequent change of scope and need of QA Engineer is for one time basis. Here the budget is fixed. If there is change in requirement or scope, then the cost would be re-estimated. Billing would be made on completion of milestones.
